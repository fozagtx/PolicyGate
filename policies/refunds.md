# Refund Policy

Standard refunds are available within 30 days of purchase when the customer provides an order reference and the item or service has not been fully consumed.

Refund requests after 30 days require manager review. A replacement, credit, or exception can be considered when the customer provides evidence of a defect, failed delivery, duplicate charge, or documented service outage.

Do not approve a high-value refund without confirming the order reference, requester identity, amount, and reason code.
